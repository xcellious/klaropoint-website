Privacy notice

How website inquiry information is handled.

Last updated: July 2026. This notice covers information submitted through klaropoint.com and does not replace customer contractual privacy terms.

Information collectedHow it is usedSharingRetentionYour choices

Information we collect

When you request a demo or contact KlaroPoint, we may collect your name, work email, organization, role, approximate claim volume, area of interest, and the message you provide.

Do not submit patient information, claim identifiers, medical details, or other protected health information through the public website form.

How we use it

  • Respond to your request
  • Evaluate whether KlaroPoint may fit your organization
  • Arrange a demonstration or security discussion
  • Improve website and inquiry processes
  • Protect against abuse and maintain service security

Service providers and sharing

The website may use service providers to host the site and transmit form submissions. KlaroPoint does not sell website inquiry information. Information may also be disclosed when required by law or necessary to protect legal rights and service security.

Retention and security

Inquiry information is retained only as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain business records, and meet legal or security obligations. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used, but no internet transmission method is completely risk-free.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of website inquiry information by contacting info@klaropoint.com. Requests may be subject to identity verification and applicable legal requirements.

This notice should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before publication, particularly once analytics, cookies, customer accounts, or additional data processors are introduced.
